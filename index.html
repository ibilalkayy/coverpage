How it works

From payment to protection in three steps

Buyer pays Cover, not the seller

Your money goes into Cover's protected hold — never touches the seller until you confirm everything is right. The seller gets a simple link to accept.

Cover monitors the transaction

We check the seller against our live scam database, verify tracking signals, and watch for fraud patterns — all in real time before and during delivery.

Confirm delivery → seller gets paid

Got your item and happy? One tap releases payment. Problem? Raise a dispute. Seller disappeared? Automatic refund. You are always protected.

Dispute? Cover arbitrates fairly

Both sides submit evidence. Cover's AI weighs trust scores, tracking data, and report history. Honest behaviour is rewarded — fraudsters can't win.

5 types of protection

Time window
90 seconds to cancel a food order. 48 hours to reconsider a non-refundable booking. You set the window.
Delivery gate
Payment releases only when the courier confirms delivery — not when the seller says it's sent. Verified by tracking API.
Approval gate
Any purchase above a set limit requires a second person to approve before money moves. Ideal for teams and families.
Milestone gate
Pay a freelancer or contractor in stages. Each tranche releases only after you verify the work at that stage is done.
Condition gate
Funds held until an external event fires — ticket validated, crowdfund goal hit, matched donation confirmed.

FAQ

Questions people always ask

Does the seller have to be registered on Cover?
No. The buyer creates the Cover-protected payment link and sends it to the seller on WhatsApp or any chat. The seller simply receives the payment through the link — no signup required from their side. This is exactly why Cover works from day one with strangers.
What if the seller refuses to use Cover?
A legitimate seller has no reason to refuse — Cover protects them too against false non-delivery claims. If a seller refuses Cover specifically, that is itself a serious red flag worth taking seriously before you pay them anything. Honest sellers welcome the trust signal Cover provides.
How does Cover hold my money — where does it go?
Your payment goes into a dedicated Cover hold account — separate from Cover's operating funds. It is not invested, not lent, and not used for anything else. It sits there until you confirm delivery, raise a dispute, or the hold window expires. You can track its status in real time.
What happens if the seller sends a fake tracking number?
Cover verifies tracking numbers directly against courier APIs — not by trusting what the seller tells us. A fake tracking number will fail verification immediately. If a seller cannot provide a valid, courier-confirmed tracking reference within the agreed window, Cover automatically returns your money.
Can I use Cover for digital products or services?
Yes — Cover uses milestone gates for digital work. A freelancer's payment is split into stages. Each stage releases only after you review and sign off the deliverable. For one-off digital purchases, Cover holds payment until you confirm you have received and can access the item.
Is Cover free to use?
Cover charges a small percentage fee per protected transaction — 1% for basic deals under Rs. 10,000, 2% for assisted deals up to Rs. 50,000, and 3% for high-value deals up to Rs. 200,000. No monthly subscription, no hidden charges. You only pay when a transaction is protected.
What happens if I receive a damaged or wrong item?
Do not confirm delivery — open a dispute instead. Upload photos of what arrived. The seller has 48 hours to respond with their own evidence. Cover's system weighs both sides and issues a verdict: full refund, partial refund, or return-then-refund. Neither side can win without evidence.
How does Cover's scam intelligence work?
Our AI continuously scans Reddit, Facebook groups, Trustpilot, government fraud registries, and our own user reports. Every seller gets a live risk score before you pay. You see the evidence — not just a number — so you can make your own informed decision. We inform, we never accuse without proof.
How long does Cover hold the money?
It depends on the hold type you choose. Time windows range from 90 seconds to 7 days. Delivery gates release automatically when the courier confirms delivery. Milestone gates release stage by stage. You always see a clear countdown and status on the Cover payment page.
Can a seller have their score reviewed if they think it's wrong?
Yes — every seller can submit a counter-claim with supporting evidence if they believe their risk score is inaccurate. Cover reviews within 72 hours. False positives hurt our credibility as much as false negatives, so we take appeals seriously and correct scores when evidence supports it.
Does Cover work for business purchases too?
Yes. Cover's Business plan adds approval chains — any purchase above a set threshold requires a manager to approve before the money moves. This prevents unauthorised spending, ad campaign errors, and supplier fraud. Contact us on WhatsApp to discuss your team's setup.
Is my financial data safe with Cover?
Cover never stores your card details — payments are processed through licensed payment gateways. Your transaction data is encrypted and never shared with third parties or used for advertising. Cover makes money from transaction fees, not your data.
